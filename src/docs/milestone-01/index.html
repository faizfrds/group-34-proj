Overview

Our project aims to develop a comprehensive productivity application tailored specifically for students from a spectrum of educational backgrounds. In today's fast-paced and digital academic environment, students often find themselves burdened by juggling a multitude of tasks, deadlines, and commitments that they simply cannot manage. The various learning management systems used complicate this problem even more as students do not have a centralized system to head to. Our application intends to alleviate this problem by providing a centralized platform for organizing and managing their academic responsibilities. By offering handy task management features, like customizable to-do lists that can be organized by the due dates, to viewing assignments by class, our app enables students to efficiently manage their assignments, ensuring that their time is spent more on their academics rather than dwelling in their stress and anxiety on managing their time. Not only does this increase student motivation, but it also boosts their motivation which aids in their overall mental well-being. Overall, our ultimate goal is to foster a healthy productivity culture within education, empowering them to achieve academic success while also maintaining a thriving state of mind.

Application Parts


Key Features of Academix:


Student Dashboard: The gateway to academic organization, the homepage displays all current classes a student is enrolled in. It allows users to easily add or remove classes, with an option to archive past classes, preserving uploaded materials while decluttering the main view. To add a class, the user simply clicks on the plus icon and enters a class name. Classes can also be personalized with unique icons the user uploads, making the experience more engaging and personalized. From here, students can navigate to the calendar to visualize their schedule or dive into specific class pages.


Individual Class Pages: The core of the class is contained here, where users will find their To-Do List, Review Section, and Materials Section:

  • -> To-Do List: Displays upcoming assignments and quizzes/exams, keeping students on track with their deadlines.
  • -> Review Section: Here students can enter the names of topics from a lecture or the lecture number itself and click an option based on their understanding (beginner, almost there, master). This allows students to keep track of everything covered in class and what material they need to focus on more, aiding in efficient exam preparation.
  • -> Materials Section: Here the user can upload files they want to store for the class, this could be anything the student deems important to the class. Lecture notes, links to study videos, anything. The result is a repository for important class materials, ensuring everything vital is just a click away.

Assignment and Material Uploads: Central to keeping academic tasks in check, this feature enables students to add assignments along with their due dates directly from the homepage or within individual class pages. Assignments are tagged to specific classes through a dropdown menu, streamlining organization. An important note, if a student adds an assignment while on a class page, the assignment is automatically tagged to that class.


Calendar View: The big picture, this dynamic calendar updates in real-time, displaying upcoming, current, and past assignments. It allows for class-specific filtering, helping students focus on one course at a time. After a student completes an assignment, they can check it off and are celebrated with confetti and a congratulatory message, fostering a positive feedback loop, and keeping them motivated.

Real-World Connection

In today’s world, students frequently struggle to manage numerous tasks from different classes effectively. This challenge to keep track of deadlines and organize assignments can lead to missed assignments, which results in stress and decreased academic performance. Traditional methods of organizing tasks, such as paper planners or basic digital calendars (iCalendar, Google Calendar, etc), often lack the specificity and customization needed to deal with the complexities of academic schedules. In addition, while some platforms are equipped to deal with these complexities (like Notion), they are hard to understand and set up, leading to frustrated users who feel like they have no choice but to give up on being organized.

To address this issue, we introduce a user-friendly website that makes homework management easy. We provide students with a centralized platform to input, organize, and monitor their assignments effectively. Our website allows students to input their homework assignments and categorize them based on class and due date. With a user-friendly interface resembling popular learning management systems such as Canvas, students can easily navigate between classes and access detailed information about each assignment. Additionally, the calendar view provides a visual representation of upcoming deadlines, allowing students to prioritize tasks effectively. In short, this is a website for students made by students.

Our website addresses a tangible real-world issue many individuals face in academic settings by providing students with a centralized platform to manage their homework assignments. Through intuitive design and customizable features, we aim to empower students to take control of their educational responsibilities and alleviate the stress associated with homework management.
