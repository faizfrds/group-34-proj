Real-World Connection

In today’s world, students frequently struggle to manage numerous tasks from different classes effectively. This challenge to keep track of deadlines and organize assignments can lead to missed assignments, which results in stress and decreased academic performance. Traditional methods of organizing tasks, such as paper planners or basic digital calendars (iCalendar, Google Calendar, etc), often lack the specificity and customization needed to deal with the complexities of academic schedules. In addition, while some platforms are equipped to deal with these complexities (like Notion), they are hard to understand and set up, leading to frustrated users who feel like they have no choice but to give up on being organized.

To address this issue, we introduce a user-friendly website that makes homework management easy. We provide students with a centralized platform to input, organize, and monitor their assignments effectively. Our website allows students to input their homework assignments and categorize them based on class and due date. With a user-friendly interface resembling popular learning management systems such as Canvas, students can easily navigate between classes and access detailed information about each assignment. Additionally, the calendar view provides a visual representation of upcoming deadlines, allowing students to prioritize tasks effectively. In short, this is a website for students made by students.

Our website addresses a tangible real-world issue many individuals face in academic settings by providing students with a centralized platform to manage their homework assignments. Through intuitive design and customizable features, we aim to empower students to take control of their educational responsibilities and alleviate the stress associated with homework management.
